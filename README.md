## 1. Introduction / المقدمة

* **Our Story / قصتنا:** Wans is more than a service; it is a human companion that restores peace of mind for seniors and reassurance for their families.
  > *"We do not just offer care... we offer life."*
  > *"مش بنقدم رعاية... بنقدم حياة."*

  Our story began when we realized the greatest challenge facing our elders is not just physical fatigue, but "Loneliness". Hence, the name "Wanas" was born. In Wanas, the story is more than a service; it started from the heart of every home. We saw the longing in the eyes of the children and the solitude in the homes of our elders, so we decided to be the bridge. We chose companions with merciful hearts, trained not only in care but also in the art of companionship and sharing.

  > *"Set your heart at rest about them... In Wanas, we don't just provide care, we provide an eye that watches over them, a heart that listens, and a companion who fills the home with laughter and stories."*
  > *"طمن قلبك عليهم.. إحنا في ونس مش بس بنقدم رعاية، إحنا بنقدم عين بتشيل، وقلب بيسمع، ورفيق يملى البيت ضحكة وحكايات."*

* **Vision / الرؤية:** To provide respectful and trusted companionship for seniors inside and outside the home while enabling families to stay reassured through continuous updates and care.
* **Mission / الرسالة:** To become the leading companion and human support service for seniors in the Arab world.
* **Core Values / القيم الأساسية:**
  * **Trust / الثقة:** We build relationships based on endless trust.
  * **Companionship / الونس:** We value being together and supporting each other.
  * **Warmth / الدفء:** We bring kindness and care into every interaction.
* **Tone of Voice / نبرة الصوت:** Warm & Reassuring (دافئة ومطمئنة):
  * **Family-like:** Comforting words that ease worries.
  * **Respectful & Dignified:** Honor elders; never imply weakness.
  * **Honest & Genuine:** Simple, sincere language without jargon.
  * **Professional & Responsible:** Accurate, safe, building endless trust.

## 2. Logo System / نظام الشعار

* **Sketch / الرسم الأولي:**
The original hand-drawn sketch captures the initial exploration of merging human forms with geometric continuity. It shows how two distinct figures—representing a senior and a companion—were thoughtfully aligned to form the infinity symbol.

<div align="center">
  <img src="https://github.com/Waelmatrix/WANAS/blob/main/assets/sketch.png?raw=true" width="600" height="500" alt="Wans Sketch">
</div>

* **Logo Concept / فكرة الشعار:**
Inspired by human connection and continuity, the logo combines the infinity symbol to reflect uninterrupted safety and boundless trust, with linking elements that represent the deep, ongoing bond between seniors and companions, emphasizing endless support and companionship.

## 4. Typography Hierarchy / التسلسل الهرمي للخطوط

- **Main Font:** Cairo
- **Secondary Font:** Tajawal

Optimized for alignment between both Arabic and English:
* **Headlines (Cairo Bold):** Used for primary titles and sections.
* **Body Text (Inter Regular):** Neutral, modern typeface for clarity.
* **CTA (Inter Medium):** Used for UI/UX buttons and highlights.
